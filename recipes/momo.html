Tibetan Momos!

Tibetan momos are made with love and community

They are typically made with beef filling but can also be found made with vegetables, potato, and chicken!

Ingredients

  • Beef
  • Scallions
  • Flour
  • Onions

Steps

  1. Prepare the dough
  2. Prepare the filling
  3. Make the momos
  4. Cook the momos in a steamer
