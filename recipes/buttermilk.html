Butter Milk

Butter Milk or Bhoe ja serves as the perfect pairing with tsampa

It is an acquired taste for some, but it is absolutely delicious for a nomad!

Ingredients

  • Black Tea
  • Butter
  • Milk
  • Water
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Bring Water to a boil
  2. Drop in the black tea bags
  3. Add in salt
  4. Mix in milk
  5. Combine all ingredients in a blender
